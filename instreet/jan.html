These inlcudes techniques like gamification, interactive videos, real time simulations, personalized and adaptive learning paths, building in-class technologies to improve both classroom and home learning experiences. This is led by people passionate for teaching as they understand process of human learning and evaluate the qualitative human coefficient keeping data in hindsight.

Perspective

We believe in the right to information. We believe information should be free, open and should be accessible to every student. Information gives the student power to choose. It is one of our founding principles that we are not going to sell content. We want to keep the content open and free. We believe selling content will only create a conflict of interest when deciding what's the best possible path for a student. The model of selling content will eventually aim at getting students and schools to buy as many courses as possible instead of trying to focus on current courses and deliver the best possible paths for them.

We believe in the idea that the traditional teaching is irreplaceable but also that it needs evolution. We believe teachers will always be needed for in-person learning and engagement, but in current education system, their full potential is not reached. In order to take complete advantages that a traditional school systems can offer, we believe there are significant changes necessary in the current system. The role of a teacher needs to focused and the teaching paradigms have to be improved. Our teaching platform reduces burden on them so that they can double down on teaching and focus on the student using personalized analysis. Using our platform, it will make the task of personalizing and accessing where a particular student is lagging behind, extremely easy. So we believe in this man+machine model to yearn the best learning experience for next generation.
